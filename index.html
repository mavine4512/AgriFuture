About Us

Our Mission

At AgriFuture, we believe that sustainable agriculture is the key to addressing global food security while preserving our planet's resources. Our mission is to promote and implement farming practices that are environmentally sound, economically viable, and socially responsible.

Founded in 2015 by Mavine Naaman from Kenya, we've worked with farmers across the country to transition to more sustainable methods, resulting in improved yields, reduced environmental impact, and stronger rural communities.

Our Values

Environmental Stewardship

We prioritize practices that protect and enhance natural resources.

Balance

Our Services

Consultation & Planning

Our expert agronomists work directly with farmers to develop customized sustainable farming plans. We assess your current practices, identify areas for improvement, and create a step-by-step transition plan that aligns with your goals and resources.

Services include soil testing, water management planning, crop rotation design, and integrated pest management strategies.

Education & Training

We offer comprehensive workshops, field days, and online courses covering all aspects of sustainable agriculture. Our educational programs are designed for farmers at all experience levels, from beginners to seasoned professionals looking to adopt new techniques.

Topics include cover cropping, conservation tillage, agroforestry, and organic certification processes.

Research & Development

Our research team continuously tests and refines sustainable farming methods. We operate demonstration farms where we evaluate new techniques, varieties, and technologies before recommending them to our partner farmers.

Current research focuses on drought-resistant crops, biological pest control, and precision agriculture technologies.

Market Access Support

We help sustainable farmers connect with markets that value their practices. Our team assists with certification processes, branding, and direct marketing strategies to ensure farmers receive fair compensation for their sustainable products.

We maintain partnerships with restaurants, grocery chains, and food cooperatives committed to sourcing from sustainable producers.
